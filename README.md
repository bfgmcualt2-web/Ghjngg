# 🃏 Blackjack Game

A fully functional web-based Blackjack game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Perfect for playing on iPad, tablets, or desktop browsers!

## Features

✅ **Complete Blackjack Rules Implementation**
- Hit/Stand mechanics
- Dealer auto-plays (must hit on 16 or less)
- Blackjack detection (21 with 2 cards)
- Bust detection
- Push/Tie handling

✅ **Player Features**

✅ **Visual Design**
- Beautiful gradient background

- iPad-optimized interface

## How to Play

1. **Place a Bet**: Enter your bet amount or use quick bet buttons ($50, $100, $500)
2. **Click Deal**: Start the game
3. **Make Decisions**:
   - **Hit**: Draw another card
   - **Stand**: End your turn (dealer plays)
4. **Win Conditions**:
   - Get 21 with 2 cards (Blackjack) = 2.5x bet
   - Get closer to 21 than dealer = 2x bet
   - Dealer busts and you don't = 2x bet
   - Push (tie) = get your bet back

## Rules

- **Blackjack** = 21 with exactly 2 cards (Ace + 10-value card)
- **Hit** = Take another card
- **Stand** = Stop taking cards
- **Bust** = Go over 21 (you lose)
- **Dealer Rules**: Dealer must hit on 16 or less, stand on 17 or more
- **Card Values**: 2-10 face value, J/Q/K = 10, A = 1 or 11
